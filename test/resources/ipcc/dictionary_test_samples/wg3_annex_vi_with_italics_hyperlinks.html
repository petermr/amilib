IPCC
Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change. See also AR6 and WG1 .
AR6
Sixth Assessment Report of the IPCC . This report includes contributions from WG1 , WG2 , and WG3 .
NDC
Nationally Determined Contribution. Parties to the Paris Agreement submit NDCs every five years.
GHG
greenhouse gas. The main GHGs are CO2 , CH4 , and N2O .
